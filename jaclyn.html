Health Services

Here, at Back to Self, we do our best to make sure every one of our clients gets the medical attention they need. Our medical services include free medical clinics for mental and physical health, and pharmacies. We work closely with RX Outreach to make sure every one of our clients has the medication they need. We also have emotional support dogs join us once a week, thanks to the New York Therapy Animals organization to help lift everyone's spirits.
