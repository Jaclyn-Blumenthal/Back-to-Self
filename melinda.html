Shelter and Food Services

Here, at Back to Self, we do our best to make sure people in need of shelter and food are taken care of. We offer both individual apartments and residential apartments to families as well as individuals that are survivors of domestic abuse and people with mental illness. We also supply food from our homegrown garden, local food co-op, and graciously donated food from The Hannaford Super Markets.

Note: If you or anyone that you know is a victim of domestic violence, click the link at the bottom of the page for help.
